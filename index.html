Crystal Collector

Crystal Number Guess!

This is a crystal collector game, I know how much you like crystals. So Here we go!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you'll add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value for each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
